What gets created
  • A new Location named Imported from <Source> (date).
  • One Floor under that location, named "Imported".
  • One Room under that floor for each unique room in your export.
  • A "Loose items" box per room. You can promote items into proper boxes later via the home view or by re-running them through the sort queue.
