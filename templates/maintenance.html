Revoke access

Sessions live in oauth2-proxy / Google, not in stash, so revocation is a server-side action:

  1. Remove the email from emails.txt and STASH_ALLOWED_EMAILS in .env.
  2. docker compose restart oauth2-proxy stash so both layers reload.
  3. For an immediate session kill, also rotate OAUTH2_PROXY_COOKIE_SECRET in .env and restart oauth2-proxy. Otherwise sessions die at the next 1h refresh.
