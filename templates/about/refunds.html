Refunds & Cancellation

Plain-English version below. This page is the canonical refund and cancellation policy — referenced from our Terms of Service.

Cancellation

You can cancel your {{ business_name }} subscription at any time from the Stripe Customer Portal, linked from the /usage page once you're signed in. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — your access stays uninterrupted until then. We do not pro-rate down to the day on cancellation; the unused time in the current period is yours to keep.

There are no cancellation fees. Re-subscribe at any time; your tenant data is preserved at the Free-tier quotas in the interim.

Refunds

{{ business_name }} is a recurring subscription service for digital household-inventory hosting. Our refund policy:

  • 14-day money-back on the first paid subscription. If you subscribe for the first time and decide within 14 days that {{ business_name }} isn't right for you, email {{ contact_email }} and we'll refund the full amount of that first charge.
  • Pro-rated refunds for service outages. If we experience an outage that prevents you from using the service for more than 24 consecutive hours, you can claim a pro-rated refund covering the affected time. Email {{ contact_email }} with the outage window.
  • No refunds on past billing cycles. Outside of the two cases above, completed billing periods are non-refundable. We don't auto-bill silently — you'll see the upgrade prompt in /usage before any charge.
  • Disputes / chargebacks. Email {{ contact_email }} *before* disputing a charge with your card issuer — almost every dispute we see is resolvable faster via direct contact than through the chargeback process.

Promotions

{{ business_name }} occasionally offers promotional codes (e.g., introductory discounts). Terms specific to each promotion are posted on this page when the promotion is active. Currently active promotions: none.

Account deletion

Cancelling your subscription does not delete your data. To permanently delete your {{ business_name }} tenant + every row and photo associated with it, contact {{ contact_email }} or use the operator-mediated tenant deletion flow described in our Privacy Policy. We honor deletion requests within 30 days; data lives on a 30-day soft-delete grace period before permanent deletion, after which it's gone from primary storage + backups.
