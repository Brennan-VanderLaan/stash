📷 Photo ingest

Drop a photo into the app. Vision AI tries to pick out every distinct item with bounding boxes — it gets most of them right, sometimes invents a "blue mug," always shows you its work so you can accept the good guesses and fix the silly ones. Beats typing names from scratch.

📦 Boxes, rooms, floors

Items live in boxes. Boxes live in rooms on a floor of a location. Sketch the floor in the in-browser editor (rect, line, label tools) or upload a real plan — either way you drag rooms onto it to mark where things live.

🏷️ QR labels

Print Avery shipping-label sheets straight from the app. Scan a label with your phone to jump to that box's contents. Optional AI-generated label art for moving-day boxes (Pro tier).

✓ Audit & rediscover

Walk a box twice a year — swipe right for "still there", left for "missing". Missing items move to a sort queue with their last known box recorded so you can track them down or accept the loss.

🔎 Find anything

Full-text + tag search across every item in your tenant. Returns the box and room so you walk to it, not search the house top-to-bottom.

⬇️ Your data, your copy

Export everything as a readable SQLite + decrypted JPEGs any time (GDPR Article 20 portability). Backup separately as an encrypted zip. No lock-in.

What we believe

  • Software that doesn't hate you. No dark patterns, no upsell carpet bombing, no quiet price hikes.
  • Cost ledger in public. Where every $ of your subscription goes — vendor bills, taxes, the human time — is on /about/transparency.
  • One operator, by design. Solo team for now. Critical paths self-service so no one waits on a support ticket to get unblocked.
  • Your inventory is yours. Per-tenant encryption at rest, exportable, deletable. No advertising, no analytics sharing.
